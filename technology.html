Innovation

Advanced Linear Generator Technology

At the forefront of sustainable energy generation, this technology converts mechanical energy directly into electrical power with minimal moving parts.

By employing a linear motion mechanism rather than traditional rotating components, this generator offers enhanced efficiency, longer operational lifespans, and greater reliability. Its compact design and scalability make it suitable for distributed energy systems, grid support, and emergency power supply.

  • Minimal moving parts — longer lifespan
  • Compact, scalable footprint
  • Distributed & grid support applications
  • Emergency & resilient power supply
Innovation

Hydrogen Injection Reactor

A cutting-edge system designed to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in transport units by using only water as the source for hydrogen production.

The reactor utilizes electrolysis to generate hydrogen on-demand, which is directly injected into the engine's combustion chamber. This seamless integration with existing engines results in optimized performance, cleaner fuel combustion, and reduced reliance on fossil fuels.

  • On-demand hydrogen from water
  • Retrofits to existing engines
  • Optimized combustion & lower emissions
  • Durable, low-maintenance design
