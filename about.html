Our History

From LNG Transport to Global Clean Energy

Amerigroup evolved from a regional LNG transporter into a global clean energy leader. Over decades, the company expanded its expertise across hydrogen production, power grid modernization, and advanced clean energy infrastructure.

Today, we operate integrated teams across Hong Kong, Beijing, and Vancouver — connecting North American innovation with Asian manufacturing capacity and Latin American energy markets.

“A Future Seamlessly Powered by Clean Energy”

Our vision: clean energy systems seamlessly integrated across sectors, enabling sustainable industrial growth, resilient communities, and zero-emission transportation networks.

Leadership

Meet Our Leadership Team

Experienced leaders driving the clean energy transition across three continents.

Mr. Gerardo Payán Ramos

President, Amerigroup International & Founder, AmeriGroup LNG • Founder, AmeriGas Propane S.A. de C.V.

Mr. Gerardo Payán Ramos serves as President of Amerigroup International and CEO of AmeriGroupLNG, as well as founder of Americanas Propane S.A. de C.V. in Mexico. With over 15 years in the energy industry, he is steering these companies toward becoming leading global providers of integrated energy solutions. His journey began as a consultant for Coca-Cola bottling companies across Latin America, where he honed his skills in electromechanical systems and quality control, transitioning to government relations between Mexico and the USA. Gerardo has become an authority in integrating emerging energy fields — encompassing an LNG system, a natural gas system, a 12-panel solar system, and a 95 location-charging app. Under his visionary leadership, these companies are continuously expanding their reach and impact on the global energy landscape.

Mr. Ken Wong

Corporate Vice President

Mr. Ken Wong, a Canadian-Chinese executive, serves as Corporate Vice President and Co-founder at Amerigroup, spearheading new business development and overseeing business operations. His entrepreneurial journey spans commodity trading and mining across Canada, the USA, and globally; notably, in 2012 he built the first logistic systems for importing iron ore from Mexico to China — a milestone recognized by SGS. Ken's business interests also encompass integrated energy solutions, capital investment, commercial real estate, enterprise supply chain management, and industrial park development. Since 2016, he has played a significant role in launching new energy systems across various countries, including Guinea, Cameroon, South Africa, and Morocco. Educated in both Computer Engineering and International Trade Business, Ken is widely respected for his leadership and strategic acumen in global business.

Mr. James Wang

CEO (China)

Mr. James Wang leads the company's strategic vision and operations, with extensive experience in building and managing international companies and leading Chinese corporations. He excels in managing government relations and positioning companies for international opportunities. He has raised approximately $1 billion and played a key role in Belt and Road projects across several African countries. A graduate in International Management from Simon Fraser University, James is also an active leader in the Hebei region, China, recognized for his strategic business expertise.

Mr. Roberto Compeán Woodworth

CFO (Mexico), Amerigroup International • CFO & Co-founder, AmeriGroupLNG

Mr. Roberto Compeán Woodworth, CFO of Amerigroup International for the Mexico region and co-founder of AmeriGroupLNG, drives the company's financial strategy with a strong background in both private and public sectors. His leadership experience includes serving as Chief Sales Officer at Mexico's National Lottery and as CFO for Bestel and Cablevisión Monterrey — key Televisa subsidiaries. Additionally, his tenure at BBVA Bank, which included advanced training in Madrid, further solidified his expertise in finance. Roberto earned his Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration from ITAM University in Mexico, underpinning his robust career in financial leadership.

Mr. Peter Pang

CFO (CGA/CFA) Global

Mr. Peter Pang, CFO of Amerigroup International, is a distinguished financial leader with over 30 years of industry experience. As a former partner at KPMG Vancouver and a Chartered Professional Accountant in Canada, he brings deep expertise in auditing, financial management, and mergers and acquisitions. Peter has been instrumental in steering major corporate mergers and guiding companies through complex financial restructuring and integration across various sectors. His strategic leadership in managing multi-billion-dollar enterprises has earned him a global reputation for enhancing profitability and delivering incisive financial insights, establishing him as one of the industry's most respected figures.

Mr. Victor Manuel Villanueva Rivera

Managing Director

Mr. Victor Manuel Villanueva Rivera is a seasoned executive with over 40 years in the automotive industry, currently serving as Managing Director in Mexico. His impressive career includes leadership roles as Managing Director at Jetour Motors and FIAT Mexico, Sales Director at Hertz-AVASA, and CEO & Managing Director of Mitsubishi Motors Mexico. With deep expertise in strategic planning, business development, quality assurance, and distributor network optimization, he has been instrumental in launching automotive brands, boosting sales, and enhancing service operations across Mexico. He holds a Master's degree in Quality Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering.

Ms. Vanessa Muñoz

Business Development Director

Ms. Vanessa Muñoz plays a key role in developing new business opportunities. She has successfully owned and operated various businesses in Mexico and the U.S., achieving top client satisfaction and awards. Previously, she was Real Estate Commercial and Leasing Manager for Liverpool. Before that, at Alsea, she facilitated new business development and strategic negotiations with major developers. Vanessa holds a Bachelor's Degree in International Relations from Tecnológico de Monterrey University in Mexico.

Ms. Livière Galván

Commercial Director

Ms. Livière Galván has been instrumental at Amerigroup, transitioning from Sales Manager to her current role as Commercial Director. In this senior leadership position, she drives the company's sales strategy, spearheading new business development and expanding Amerigroup's portfolio. Prior to joining Amerigroup, she served as Director of Sales and Marketing at Oronames for over five years. Livière holds both a Master of Science and a Bachelor's degree in Marketing.
